About us

Shuddhi Trust is a volunteer-run non-profit organization based in Chennai, India. We are committed to doing what is necessary with transparency, efficiency and inclusivity.

Founded on 30th March 2017, we are a registered Public Charitable Trust as of 4th February 2021.

Note - We were founded as Shuddhi Fund, but have since renamed the organisation to Shuddhi Trust to avoid confusion regarding our registration status. We may not have been able to alter all references to Shuddhi Fund in our earlier publications.
